Overview

Lumina is a next-generation lighting control platform designed for architectural spaces. We partnered with Lumina Labs to create a brand identity that captures the intersection of precision engineering and ambient atmosphere.

Challenge

The lighting industry is saturated with clinical, overly technical branding. Lumina needed to differentiate itself by communicating both technical sophistication and emotional warmth — the feeling of perfectly lit spaces.

  • Stand out in a crowded B2B market
  • Appeal to both architects and facility managers
  • Translate complex technology into accessible visuals

Solution

We developed a visual system centered on gradients that mimic natural light transitions — from warm amber to cool daylight. The typography pairs geometric precision with subtle organic curves, reflecting the balance between control and comfort.

The result is a brand that feels both premium and approachable, with a flexible identity system that scales from mobile interfaces to large-format environmental graphics.
